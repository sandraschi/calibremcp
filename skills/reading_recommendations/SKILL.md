# Reading Recommendations

**Description:** Get personalized reading recommendations from your Calibre library using series progress, ratings, tags, and reading history.

## Steps

1. Use prompt **reading_recommendations** or ask "What should I read next?"
2. Tools: `query_books` to list unread or by tag/series; `manage_analysis` for reading statistics; `manage_metadata` for details.
3. Chain: list unread → filter by rating/series → recommend top picks with reasoning.

## Example

"Recommend my next read from unread fantasy with high ratings."
